LongReach AI is dedicated to transparency, data privacy, and security, and we aim to comply with applicable data protection laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (“CCPA”), the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (“LGPD”), and the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (“GDPR”).

This Policy applies only to information that we collect through or in connection with our Apps and Services. If you have any concerns, please contact us using the methods in the “Contact Us” section below. If you do not agree with your information being used as described in this Policy, please do not install or use the Services.

2. Information We Collect and How We Collect It

While using our Services, we may ask you to provide certain Personal Data, and we may also collect some information automatically or via third-party partners. The categories of data we collect include:

2.1 Basic Identifiers and Contact Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, for example when you contact us via email, support forms, or in-app help features. This may include:

  • Name or nickname
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if you choose to provide it)
  • Any other information you choose to include in your messages

2.2 Device Information

When you download and use our Services, we automatically collect certain device-related information, such as:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • Screen resolution
  • App version and installation source (e.g., Google Play, Apple App Store)
  • Device identifiers (for example, device ID, advertising ID such as IDFA or GAID, IDFV, Android ID)
  • Language settings, time zone, and country
  • IP address (which may be used to infer approximate location)

2.3 Usage Information

We collect information about your activity within the Services (“Usage Information”), such as:

  • Date and time you use the Services
  • Features and screens you access, session length, and gameplay events
  • In-app purchase history and subscription status
  • Your interaction with advertisements (e.g., views, clicks)
  • Game progress, levels, achievements, and in-game items
  • Crash logs, performance metrics, and diagnostics

2.4 Location Information (Non-Precise)

When you download and use our Services, we automatically detect broad, non-precise location such as country and state via your IP address. With your consent where required by law, we may also infer approximate city-level location.

We do not collect precise GPS-based location unless clearly indicated and you have explicitly granted permission.

2.5 Media and Other Files

Certain optional features may require access to your device’s camera, microphone, photo library, or storage, for example:

  • Capturing screenshots, avatars, or gameplay moments
  • Uploading images or files when using in-app support or feedback

If you grant such permissions, we will only access the media/files needed for the specific feature, and only while you are using that feature. You can revoke these permissions at any time via your device settings.

2.6 Information We Obtain from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party service providers, such as analytics providers, advertising networks, attribution and measurement partners, and platform providers, in accordance with their own privacy policies. This can include:

  • Aggregated or pseudonymous analytics data
  • Attribution information (e.g., if you installed our App after clicking an ad)
  • Approximate location or device type inferred by those partners

2.7 Information We Obtain When You Connect with Third-Party Accounts

If we offer the option to connect or log in via third-party services (such as Apple Game Center, Google Play Games, or Facebook) and you choose to do so, we may receive information from those platforms depending on their settings and your permissions, for example:

  • Apple ID or Game Center player ID
  • Google Play Games profile name or gamer tag
  • Facebook user ID, name, profile picture, and email (if you allow it)

For details on how these third parties process your data or how to manage your settings, please see their respective privacy policies and account settings.

2.8 Information We Obtain from Facebook and Other Social Platforms

If you use our Services on or through Facebook, or connect your game to your Facebook account or other social network, we may receive the information you permit that platform to share, such as:

  • User ID or profile information
  • Your connections or friends who also play our games (as allowed by the platform)
  • Information about your interactions with our game on that platform

The information we receive from these third parties is stored and used in accordance with this Policy.

2.9 User-Generated Content

We may collect Content you voluntarily provide or post, including:

  • In-game chat messages or comments (if available in a game)
  • Posts, images, or videos you share with us via social media or directly
  • Your name, username, profile picture, voice, and other identifiable information contained in that content

We will process such content in line with this Policy and any applicable consent requirements.

2.10 AI-Generated Content (AIGC) and Generative Features

Some of our games or Services may include AI-powered or AIGC Content features. These may include in-game characters, items, levels, text, artwork, dialogue, effects, or other creative elements based on your inputs or your interactions within the game.

Depending on the specific feature, we may process:

  • Text prompts, commands, or selections you input
  • Images, avatars, or artwork you upload (if applicable)
  • Voice or audio samples, if you explicitly enable microphone- or voice-based features
  • Choices and interactions you make while using AI-powered features

AI processing may occur on-device or on secure cloud servers operated by us or by our service providers. We use your prompts, inputs, and interactions solely to generate the requested in-game output, perform necessary safety checks, maintain AIGC quality, and protect against abuse. We do not use your Personal Data or AIGC inputs to train public or unrelated third-party AI models, nor do we sell AIGC-related data to advertisers.

In addition to user-facing AIGC features, some of our internal game assets are also created using AI tools. These internally generated creatives may include:

  • AI-generated icons, UI elements, illustrations, and graphic artwork
  • AI-generated photos, backgrounds, materials, and textures
  • AI-generated sound effects, ambient audio, music, or character voices
  • AI-assisted animations, procedural levels, or in-game environmental elements
  • Other AI-enhanced creative assets used in game production

These internal AIGC assets are produced without using any user Personal Data and are fully owned by LongReach AI. Unless explicitly permitted, users may not extract, reuse, or distribute AI-generated internal game assets outside of our Services.

AIGC outputs may be automatically or manually reviewed for the purposes of safety, quality, moderation, and legal compliance. Outputs that are harmful, unlawful, or violate our policies may be modified, restricted, or removed. AIGC features may also be updated, limited, or discontinued at any time as necessary for safety or operational reasons.

We and our third-party advertising and analytics partners may use cookies and similar technologies (such as mobile SDKs) to provide, personalize, and analyze our Services and to display advertising.

These technologies may:

  • Identify your device across apps and sites (for example, via an advertising ID)
